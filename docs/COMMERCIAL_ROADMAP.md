# Commercial Roadmap

## Demo Scope

- Public Web/PWA demo.
- Talk, Breathe, Move, Timeline, Me pages.
- Mock AI understanding and recommendations.
- Mock calm scripts and gentle movement plans.
- Local storage record timeline.
- Device simulator.
- Chinese-first, mixed-language tolerant copy.
- Clear health disclaimer.

## Post-Hackathon Product Work

- Real AI Agent orchestration with prompt/version management.
- RAG service for user history, content library, and safety rules.
- Authentication and user account lifecycle.
- Database-backed long-term records.
- Privacy center, consent records, export, and delete.
- Real voice input and TTS.
- Bilingual UI and prompt dictionaries for Chinese, English, and mixed usage.
- Hardware firmware protocol and Bluetooth/Wi-Fi adapters.
- Permission-based GPS active sharing.
- Caregiver sharing that is user-initiated, transparent, and revocable.
- Subscription packaging and clinical safety review.

## Explicit Non-Goals

- Medical diagnosis.
- Drug or supplement prescription.
- Emergency response.
- Covert monitoring.
- Replacing clinicians or therapists.
